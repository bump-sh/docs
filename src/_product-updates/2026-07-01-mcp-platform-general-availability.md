The Bump.sh MCP Platform is now generally available, **on all plans**. 

### What was added during the beta
- [Arazzo support](/help/mcp-servers/specification-support/arazzo-support/): alongside our own [Flower](/help/mcp-servers/specification-support/flower-support/) specification, you can now run Arazzo workflows on your MCP servers.
- [Custom OAuth](/help/mcp-servers/access-management/#private-mcp-servers-using-your-own-oauth-server): let your users authenticate to your MCP server using your own identity provider (Okta, Auth0, Keycloak, etc.) instead of a Bump.sh account. 
- [Custom domains](/help/mcp-servers/customization-options/custom-domain/): have your MCP server under your own domain, as you do for your docs, for a consistent experience. 

Any questions or feedback? [Reach out to us](mailto:hello@bump.sh).
